Welcome! Our office is responsible for certifying VA benefits to the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs, as well as administering the Texas Hazlewood Act Exemption and processing waiver and exemption programs at The University of Texas at Dallas.
For questions regarding the Hazlewood Act Exemption, please contact Kimberly Bomnskie at veteransbenefits@utdallas.edu or by calling 972-883-4020.
For questions regarding the certification process for VA benefits, please contact Kimberly Bomnskie at veteransbenefits@utdallas.edu or via phone at 972-883-4020.
If you are unsure of your eligibility for VA benefits, please contact the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs by calling 1-888-GIBILL-1 (1-888-442-4551) or use the Ask VA feature. The University of Texas at Dallas is unable to determine an individual’s eligibility for VA educational benefits.
For more information on how to qualify for the Texas Hazlewood Act Exemption, please visit the Texas Veterans Commission website.
