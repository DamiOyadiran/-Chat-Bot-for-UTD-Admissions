Effective September 1, 2005, Sandia National Laboratories employees, as well as their spouses and dependent children, may pay the tuition and fees charged to residents of the state of Texas when enrolled in a UT System institution. This waiver of out-of-state tuition terminates upon the termination of employment with Sandia. Documentation of eligibility will be required of enrolled students at the beginning of each semester in which they are enrolled in a UT System institution.
Email the Office of Financial Aid to begin the application process.
