Veteran Exemptions and Waivers
Veteran Benefits Forms

The purpose of this program is to permit a small number of individuals who are citizens of Mexico and who have financial need to enroll in any public university in Texas while paying the resident tuition rate.
In order to be eligible for the waiver, you must:

Be a citizen of Mexico and reside in Mexico.
Demonstrate financial need.

Applications must be completed online via the International Students and Scholars Office website.
