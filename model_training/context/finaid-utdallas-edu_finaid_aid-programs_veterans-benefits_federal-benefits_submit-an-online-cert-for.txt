Complete a Certification for Federal Veteran Benefits Form

To fill out the online certification request form, navigate to the forms page. Select the appropriate request to fill out beneath the ‘Federal Forms’ section.

Use your NetID and Password to login.

Once logged in, fill out the form in its entirety and click ‘Submit’. NOTE: The ‘Submit’ button only becomes available once all necessary fields are completed.

You will only be able to fill out this form and submit it once. Any changes made after submission should be sent to us via email: veteransbenefits@utdallas.edu
