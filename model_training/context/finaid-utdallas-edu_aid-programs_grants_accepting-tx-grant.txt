Login to Galaxy at galaxy.utdallas.edu

Use your Netid to login

Click on the Orion Student Center link.

Access Your To Do List

Once you are in the Student Center, click on the To Do List – More link to access your To Do List.

Select the TEXAS Grant Statement of Eligibility

Carefully Review the Program Eligibility Information
Select “Yes” or “No” in order to answer the eligibility question.
Click “Save” and then “Next.”

Click “Finish” to submit the TEXAS Grant Statement of Eligibility.

If you have any questions about this process, please contact the Office of Financial Aid at financial-aid@utdallas.edu or at 972-883-2941
