The purpose of this program is to provide financial assistance to students who once were in foster or other residential care and have been adopted. The exemption covers the tuition and fees for courses for which UT Dallas receives state tax support.
In order to be eligible for the exemption, you must have been:

In foster or other residential care.
Adopted.
The subject of an adoption assistance agreement under Subchapter D, Chapter 162 of the Texas Family Code.

Provide the Office of Financial Aid with written proof of eligibility from the Texas Department of Family and Protective Services (TDFPS). To obtain a letter of eligibility from TDFPS, contact a representative in one of the district offices:

Adoption and Foster Care
Preparation for Adult Living (PAL)

Contact the Department of Family and Protective Services or your adoption assistance case worker for additional information.
Per House Bill 452, 82nd Legislature, assistance locating temporary housing between academic terms (fall, spring, and summer) may be available to eligible students who were in foster care on the day preceding their eighteenth birthday and who were enrolled full-time at a public institution in the previous term and are registered or committed to enrolling full-time at a public institution in the subsequent term.
