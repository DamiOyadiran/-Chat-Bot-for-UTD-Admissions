The purpose of this program is to enable an individual and his or her spouse and children in the state in accordance with the agreement between the parties to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization to enroll in public institutions in Texas while paying the resident tuition rate.
This program permits nonresident students to pay tuition and fees at the resident rate. Contact the Veterans Benefits Team to begin the application process. Students must provide proof that the family is stationed in Texas in keeping with the North Atlantic Treaty.
